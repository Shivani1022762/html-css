Expert divers have faced gray areas, and they overcame them almost always instinctively.
But some divers need to learn what gray areas are, and how to overcome plateaus and adversities.
Traditional courses do not teach divers how to systematically and methodically progress after the conclusion of the course itself, nor how to deal actively with stress, fear, and technical limitations.

Setbacks in diving skills progression and poor feeling can ruin our dives, or in extreme cases, even push us to quit diving.

Our goal is to help SCUBA divers overcome plateaus in their diving skills progression, and when present, stress, fear, and technical limitations.
